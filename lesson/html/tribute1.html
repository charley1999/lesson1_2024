Benifits to Christmas

Some benifits to Christmas are that we get gifts from our loved ones and get to have fun with family and friends.

Downfall to Christmas

Money.

Money is a big issue for those who work minimum wage job and are struggling with day to day life.

  • Lack of money to get family the gift they want.
  • choosing how much money to spend on everyone!

Family and Friends.

Family and Friends can be fun but sometimes if your low on money it can be a big burden to find something for everyone.

  • Lack of friends and family.
  • Friends and Family are in a different state or country!

How we can bring hope to others during Christmas.

All in all Christmas has good and bad things about it but always do your best to keep the spirit of Christmas alive in the ways that are available to you.
